INDEPENDENT RESEARCH EXPERIENCE

Regioselective Synthesis of 4-Functionalized Pyridines

07/2020-06/2021

Supervisor: Dr. Song Song and Prof. Ning Jiao
  • Developed an aromatic C-4 selective functionalization of pyridine N-oxides with DABCO as the regioselective controller.
  • N-(4-pyridtl)-DABCO salt was the key intermediate, enabling C-Cl, C-Br, and C-F bond formation.

Synthesis of Furoxan and Isoxazole Compounds using Sodium Nitrite as Nitrogen Source

06/2019-07/2020

Supervisor: Dr. Song Song and Prof. Ning Jiao
  • Devised a method for constructing furoxan and isoxazole derivatives. Widely-available inorganic salt NaNO2 was used as a nitrogen source, thereby expanding new strategies for organic synthesis.

HFIP-Promoted Electrophilic Sulfenylation and Trifluoromethylthiolation of Aromatic Compounds

03/2019-06/2019

Supervisor: Dr. Song Song and Prof. Ning Jiao
  • Developed a method to achieve sulfenylation and trifluoromethylthiolation of aromatics, HFIP can serve as a solvent to obtain the target product under catalyst-free conditions.
  • Applied this method to the late-stage functionalization of small bioactive molecules.
 

COLLABORATIVE RESEARCH EXPERIENCE

Electrophilic Halogenation of Electron-Deficient Aromatic Rings

04/2019-06/2019

Core member, supervised by Dr. Song Song and Prof. Ning Jiao

Copper-Catalyzed Aromatic Ring Cleavage Reaction

10/2018-03/2019

Core member, supervised by Dr. Song Song and Prof. Ning Jiao
  • This work was a copper-catalyzed reaction converting anilines into alkenyl nitriles by selective C-C bond cleavage.

Nitrogen-Substituted Carbene-Catalyzed Synthesis of Esters Using Oxygen in Air as the Oxygen Source

10/2018-12/2018

Core member, supervised by Dr. Song Song and Prof. Ning Jiao
  • This work was a novel N-heterocyclic carbene catalyzed oxidative transformation of organic halides into esters through the incorporation of O-atom from O2.
  • Synthesized a series of benzyl bromides and explored the substrate scope when reacting with aliphatic alcohols under standard experimental conditions.
